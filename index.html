Jan Shiksha Evam Sanskar Samiti is a registered non-governmental organization(NGO) working in Vasundhara Ghaziabad, India.

About Us

Jan Shiksha Evam Sanskar Samiti is a registered non-governmental organization(NGO) working in Vasundhara Ghaziabad, India. It got registered in January 2013 but was operating since October 2010.

The first centre was opened in sector 18 in September 2010 with about eight to ten students, with the goal of providing free education to all children from 5 to 14 years old. Since then, six additional centres have been opened in the different sectors of the area with more than 350 students. Most of the children who are linked with the organization belong to the deprived section of society. In the majority of cases, their parents make their living as vegetable and fruit vendors, labourers, rikshaw drivers and maids.

Due to the lack of government schools in Vasundhara and a lack of awareness of the importance of education, most of these children do not receive an education and hence are deprived of their fundamental rights. In a child-friendly environment using child-centred methods, Jan Shiksha Evam Sanskar Samiti provides children with the opportunity to learn, unlearn, and re-learn in a safe environment ensuring no child is left behind.

Our Mission

We are working towards a child-caring and child-supportive society where every child gets the opportunity to receive education in a safe environment. So that they could live with dignity, hope and comfort. The mission of our organization is to provide appropriate guidance and support for children from economically weaker and socially deprived sections, so they can develop physically, emotionally, socially, and spiritually.

Aim and Objectives

Jan Shiksha Evam Sanskar Samiti's aim and objectives.

  • To promote education among the educationally backward communities, particularly in slum areas, migrant workers.
  • Educate deprived sections of society about the importance of education.
  • To help in the enrollment of students in the formal education system
  • To arrange education including value-based education.
  • To encourage sponsors and facilitate better education.
  • To promote a sense of cleanliness, hygiene, health, value and service in children.
  • To prepare and promote children for better future opportunities and healthy lifestyles.
  • To empower youth and women for a better quality of life and improve, the living health conditions of society, and economically, deprived populations.
  • To install Gandhian education style in young minds.

Our Values

JSSS works on some inhrerent values and beliefs.

Aim for the highest professional and ethical standards of education to children with child-centred methods and a safe child-friendly environment.

Respect and value the knowledge, skills and contributions of all in the organization and the community.

Respect the rights, choices and confidentiality of children and their families.

Maintain transparency in interactions with children, donors and well-wishers.
